You can trust us with the line-up.

Izvir is a festival held by Formaviva, the platform and organization founded two decades ago with a mission to facilitate and empower independent music and its creators.

During the 2 days of the festival, we will experience a meticulously curated lineup of visionary artists that will take you on a journey through sound, from deep ambient to the melodic vibrations of house and the pulsing rhythms techno, some of the best indepedent electronic music unbeknownst to the world.

But the music is just one aspect of what makes Izvir special. It's the people, the energy, and the spirit of the community that truly set it apart. We come together from all corners of the world to celebrate life, dance, and embrace the beauty of our diversity.

For these reasons, the festival has been held back with promotion and reserved until this 9th year for a very limited and intimate group of people who became its foundation — a part of an extended family.
